# Product Definition & Requirements (PDR)
## Product: FOREVER Landing Page (Japan-Aligned MVP)
Version: 0.2  
Scope: One landing page. Education-first.  
Status: Revised for Japan market.

---

## 1. Objective

Build a **single, high-clarity landing page** that educates first-time users, establishes trust, and drives them to **one primary lead-capture action**, followed by a secondary purchase path.

**Primary goal:**  
→ User submits their email to receive an educational guide (lead capture).

**Secondary goal:**  
→ User proceeds to product checkout after education.

---

## 2. Target User

- One primary persona (defined in one sentence)
- First-time or lightly aware visitor
- Primarily mobile
- Risk-averse, detail-oriented (Japan market)

---

## 3. Page Structure (Required Order)

Mobile-first, vertical scroll only:

1. **Hero**
   - Credibility-focused headline
   - Soft, informational CTA

2. **Trust & Process**
   - Manufacturing, longevity, quality systems

3. **Problem / Context**
   - Why typical solutions are insufficient

4. **Solution**
   - What FOREVER is
   - How quality and process differ

5. **Proof**
   - Longevity, usage context, factual credibility

6. **Primary Offer (Lead Capture)**
   - Educational PDF guide
   - Email-only form

7. **Secondary CTA**
   - Product list or checkout link

8. **Footer**
   - Privacy / Terms
   - Company information

---

## 4. Content Rules

- Headline = clarity over persuasion
- Neutral, factual tone
- One primary CTA (lead capture)
- Secondary CTA visually de-emphasized

**Example CTA:**  
“Get the Free Aloe Guide”

---

## 5. Design Requirements

- Mobile-first layout
- Minimalist Japanese aesthetic
- Clean typography
- High contrast only for primary CTA
- No urgency language

---

## 6. Technical Requirements

- Stack: HTML/CSS or lightweight framework
- Hosting: Netlify or Vercel
- Forms: Simple email capture
- Basic SEO

---

## 7. Performance Requirements

- Load time under 2 seconds
- Compressed assets

---

## 8. Legal & Tracking

- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Pageviews and form submissions

---

## Definition of Done

- Page is live
- Trust sections clearly explain process and quality
- Lead capture works end-to-end
- Secondary purchase path is accessible
- Data is collected
