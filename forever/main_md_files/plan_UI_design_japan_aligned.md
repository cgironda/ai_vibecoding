# GUI Plan: FOREVER Landing Page (Japan-Aligned MVP)

**Basis:** `master_plan_simple_Japan_Aligned.md`, `PDR_Japan_Aligned.md`  
**Philosophy:** Calm, Informational, Trust-First (Japan UX norms)

---

## 1. Visual Identity (Japan Adjustment)

### Typography

Japanese readability and neutrality take priority over brand expressiveness.

- **Primary Font (Headings):**  
  `Noto Sans JP`, `Hiragino Kaku Gothic ProN`, or `system-ui`

- **Secondary Font (Body):**  
  Same family as headings for consistency

- **Weight Usage:**  
  - Headings: Medium–SemiBold (600 max)
  - Body: Regular (400)

**Avoid:** Extra-bold weights. They reduce perceived credibility in Japan.

---

### Type Scale

- `H1` (Hero): 2.2rem (Mobile) / 3rem (Desktop)
- `H2`: 1.6rem (Mobile) / 2.2rem (Desktop)
- Body: 1rem–1.05rem

Tighter hierarchy than Western pages.

---

## 2. Color Palette (Japan)

- **Background:** `#FFFFFF` or `#FAFAFA`
- **Primary Text:** `#1F2937` (Soft near-black)
- **Secondary Text:** `#6B7280`
- **Accent / CTA:** Muted green or calm blue (no red urgency)
  - Example: `#2F855A` or `#2563EB`
- **Borders:** `#E5E7EB`

**Rule:** Contrast without aggression.

---

## 3. Layout & Structure (Mobile-First)

### Vertical Stack (Required)

1. Hero
2. Trust / Process
3. Context / Problem
4. Solution
5. Proof
6. Lead Capture
7. Secondary CTA
8. Footer

Spacing is **more generous** than global version.

---

## 4. Microcopy Rules (Critical)

### Headline Style

- Declarative, not promotional
- Emphasize continuity and quality

**Examples (Structure):**
- 「毎日の生活に取り入れられるアロエベラの栄養」
- 「40年以上続く品質管理と製造プロセス」

---

### CTA Microcopy

Primary CTA (Lead Capture):
- 「無料ガイドを見る」
- 「資料を受け取る」

Secondary CTA:
- 「製品一覧を見る」
- 「詳しくはこちら」

**Avoid:**
- 今すぐ
- 限定
- お得

---

## 5. Components

### Buttons

- Shape: Slightly rounded (`6px` radius)
- Padding: Generous, but visually light
- No hover animations beyond subtle shade change

---

### Forms

- Email only
- Clear privacy reassurance below field

**Example:**  
「ご入力いただいた情報は、資料送付以外の目的では使用しません。」

---

### Images

- Product images preferred over lifestyle
- Neutral lighting, no exaggerated emotion
- Rounded corners optional but subtle

---

## 6. Accessibility (Japan Expectations)

- Clear focus states
- High text readability
- Adequate line spacing (1.6–1.7)

---

## 7. What This UI Explicitly Avoids

- Aggressive contrast
- Red CTA buttons
- Animated urgency cues
- Dense text blocks
- Western-style hype layouts

---

## Final Note

This UI plan is intentionally conservative.  
In Japan, **visual restraint increases trust**.

If it feels "too quiet," that is usually correct for this market.
